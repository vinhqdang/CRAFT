\begin{abstract}
Explainability for multi-modal autonomous perception has emerged as a critical research gap. We introduce CRAF-X, a Cross-modal Robust Adaptive Fusion with eXplainability framework. By explicitly mapping spatial modality contradictions, we derive a dynamic Gated Adaptive Fusion Module (GAFM) bounded by an Adversarial Consistency Training (ACT) routine. CRAF-X represents the first multi-modal Bird's-Eye View (BEV) fusion framework extracting geometric consistency intrinsically to suppress adversarial signals and produce spatially grounded interpretation maps.
\end{abstract}
